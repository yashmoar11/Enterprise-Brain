# TechFin Corp - Q3 2025 Earnings Report

**Date**: October 15, 2025
**Ticker**: TFC

## Executive Summary
TechFin Corp reported a strong Q3 with revenue of $1.2 Billion, a 15% increase year-over-year. The growth was primarily driven by the acquisition of **DataFlow Systems**, a cloud analytics provider.

## Key Financials
*   **Revenue**: $1.2B (up 15% YoY)
*   **Net Income**: $250M
*   **EPS**: $3.45

## Strategic Acquisitions
On August 1, 2025, TechFin Corp completed the acquisition of **DataFlow Systems** for $450 Million. 
**Sarah Chen**, the former CEO of DataFlow Systems, has been appointed as the new **Chief Technology Officer (CTO)** of TechFin Corp.

## Risks
The company faces regulatory scrutiny regarding its new AI lending platform, "LoanBot". The SEC has opened an inquiry into potential bias in the algorithm.
